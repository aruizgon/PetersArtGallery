CHA Trip

Hi There! We are the developers behind Peter's Art Collection! We are students enrolled in Informatics 161 (Project Management) here at UCI. For our project, we noticed that there are many artistic students here at UCI. Our goal is to provide a platform, an area for them to post their work for free, get in contact with other UCI student artists, and have this on their resume. We want everyone to just have fun posting their precious work and enjoy what UCI students have to offer :))
